Our Mission

Built for the people who build hackathons

University hackathon organizers are builders, not administrators — but today's tooling forces them to become both. HackSuite exists to eliminate the operational friction so your team can focus on what actually matters: creating an experience that inspires students to build.

We believe every hackathon deserves infrastructure as serious as its ambitions. HackSuite is purpose-built for university teams — from 50-person local events to 1,000+ attendee flagship hackathons.
