# Prompts

## Vision Document — Hackathon OS

Write a comprehensive product vision document for a platform called **HackSuite** — a purpose-built SaaS tool designed exclusively for university hackathon organizers.

### Problem to Solve
Today's hackathon organizers cobble together a fragile stack of disconnected tools: Google Sheets for registrations, Notion for internal docs, Devpost for submissions, separate spreadsheets for judging scores, email threads for sponsor coordination, and manual check-in flows on event day. This creates duplicated data, constant context-switching, and error-prone handoffs at exactly the moments when organizers can least afford mistakes.

### What to Build
Describe a unified platform that manages the **full hackathon lifecycle** — from the first registration link going live to the post-event sponsor report — in a single, coherent product.

### Requirements to Address

**Platform scope:**
- Support both MLH-affiliated hackathons and independent university hackathons
- Multi-tenant architecture: one organization account per hackathon club/team, with multiple organizer roles and permission levels per team
- Mobile-first UI, since organizers are on their feet during event day and need to act quickly from a phone

**Core modules to define** (describe each module, its key features, and how it connects to adjacent modules):
1. **Registration & Applications** — custom forms, acceptance workflows, waitlists, confirmation emails
2. **Check-in & Attendance** — day-of QR check-in, real-time headcount, dietary/accommodation tracking
3. **Schedule & Logistics** — agenda builder, workshop/room assignments, push notifications to attendees
4. **Project Submissions & Judging** — submission portal, judge assignment, scoring rubrics, live leaderboard
5. **Sponsor Management** — tier tracking, deliverable checklists, logo/asset library, post-event metrics report
6. **Finance & Budgeting** — budget planning, expense tracking, reimbursement requests, sponsor payment reconciliation, export-ready financial summaries for university accounting
7. **Marketing & Outreach** — email campaign builder, social media copy generation, registration page customization, attendee referral tracking, hype metrics dashboard
8. **Post-Event Reporting** — attendance analytics, demographic breakdowns, judge feedback summaries, sponsor-ready recap export

**Differentiation:**
- Explain concretely what makes HackSuite execute meaningfully better than the current duct-tape approach
- Identify two or three "10x moments" where the platform saves organizers significant time or prevents a real failure mode (e.g., double-booked judges, lost check-in data, missed sponsor deliverable)

### Output Format
Structure the vision document with:
- An executive summary (3–5 sentences)
- A problem statement with specific pain points
- A module-by-module breakdown with descriptions and inter-module connections
- A competitive positioning section (vs. Devpost, HackDash, or just generic tools)
- A closing "why now" section explaining the market timing
